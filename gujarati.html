Thepla recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 cup whole wheat flour
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 tomato, chopped
  • 2 tsp gram flour
  • oil or ghee for cooking
  • ½ tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1 tbsp curd,water
  • Salt to taste
  • Chopped coriander for garnish

    • Instructions:

  • Heat oil, sauté onions until golden brown.
  • Add tomatoes, spices, and cook well.
  • Mix flour,semolina,carom seeds,salt. Add melted ghee,Gradually add warm water to form stiff dough.Rest for 15-20 mins.
  • Serve hot Thepla.
  • Enjoy your delicious Gujarati meal...
