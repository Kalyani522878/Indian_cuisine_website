Masala Dosa

Ingrediants

For Dosa Batter..

  • 3 cups of Rice
  • 1 cup of Urad dal
  • 1 tsp fenugreek seeds
  • Salt to taste
  • Butter or Ghee for authentic taste

    • For delicious Potato Masala

  • Boiled potatoes
  • Onion (sliced)
  • Green chilli (chopped)
  • 1/2 tsp mustard seeds
  • 5-6 curry leaves
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • salt to taste
  • 1 tbsp oil

    • Instructions:

  • Soak rice, urad dal, and fenugreek seeds for 6 hours.
  • Grind to a smooth batter and ferment overnight.
  • Heat oil in a pan, add mustard seeds, curry leaves, onions, and chilies & saute it.
  • Add mashed potatoes, turmeric, and salt.
  • Mix well and your authentic potato masala is ready.
  • Heat a dosa pan, pour batter, spread thinly, and drizzle butter or ghee.
  • Cook until golden brown, place potato masala inside, and fold.
  • Serve hot with chutney and sambar.
