Chumchum recipe

Ingredients

  • 1l milk, 2tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 cup sugar, 4cups water
  • 1/2 tbsp cardamom powder
  • pistachios, grated coconut(optional)

    • Instructions:

  • curdle milk with lemon juice, strain and knead paneer.
  • shape into ovals, boil in sugar syrup for 15 mins.
  • cool, soak in rose water, garnish and serve.
